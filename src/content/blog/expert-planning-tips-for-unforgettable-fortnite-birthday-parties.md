If your kid is obsessed with Fortnite, why not surprise them with a Fortnite-themed birthday party? With a few clever decorating tips, exciting party favors, and the perfect entertainment, you can easily plan an unforgettable party that your child and all their friends will be sure to love.

At AFK Mobile Arcade, we've been delighted to [host numerous Fortnite birthday parties](/birthday-parties/) throughout the community, and we know what can take an ordinary event to the next level. Below, we'll share some key tips to make your party a smash success.

- **Set the theme with Fortnite invitations:** Get the party started on the right foot weeks before the festivities begin with themed invitations. You can get invitations styled like loot boxes, Fortnite loading screens, or battle passes, immediately getting guests excited for the upcoming party.

- **Plan a complementing color scheme:** Fortnite prominently features bold colors like royal blue, vibrant purple, and neon green, so consider getting balloons, banners, and tableware in these signature shades to create an immersive environment.

- **Prepare themed snacks:** With a little creativity, everyday snacks can be transformed into treats that will remind guests of their favorite game. For instance, blue Gatorade can be served in transparent cups to resemble the game's Chug Jugs. Similarly, you can create loot box treasure chests stuffed with candies or cookies.
