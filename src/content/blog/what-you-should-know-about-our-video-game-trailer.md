With mobile and video gaming being so popular, it only makes sense that mobile arcades would become popular as well. We at AFK Mobile Arcade offer a fun [video game trailer](/game-library/) that can be booked for any of your events, whether it's a school or church event, after-school activity, work gathering, birthday party, or otherwise. Here are a few things you should know about our video game trailer.

- **Perfect for large groups:** Our video game trailer is perfect for large groups. It can seat up to 23 players and has sufficient space for any of your gaming needs, including virtual reality setups.

- **Fitted with a variety of games and gaming systems:** Our [video game trailer](/game-library/) is also fitted with a variety of games and gaming systems that people of all ages can enjoy. We have all the latest gaming consoles, the most popular multiplayer games, and games of all types, including adventure, racing, sports, and more. You can also notify us of any favorite games before the event, so we can make them readily available or come prepared with similar recommendations.

- **Climate controlled:** You can enjoy events in our video game trailer in any weather conditions. The trailer is climate-controlled, so you and your guests can enjoy comfortable conditions as you play, even if it's cold and raining or hot and uncomfortable outside.

- **Requires space to park:** We handle nearly everything when you book our video game trailer for your event, but we will need space to park. We will need about 50–60 feet of space on level ground. No external hookups are required.
