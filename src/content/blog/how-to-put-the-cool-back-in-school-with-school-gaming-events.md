If your students are bored with your usual after-school events, you might consider a school gaming event. We at AFK Mobile Arcade can help you with that. We can provide the games, space, and experiences — as long as you can provide a spot to park, the students, and teacher or adult supervision. Here are a few ways that [school gaming events can bring excitement to your after-school events](/school-events/).

- **Help kids come out of their shells:** School gaming events can help kids break out of their shells by creating a fun environment where they can play video games with other students. We supply all the most popular multiplayer games, so students will have to work together or take part in friendly competitions and form lasting friendships.

- **Parents and teachers can get involved too:** Parents and teachers can join in the gaming as well. We can stock your school gaming event with new favorites as well as classic games that parents and teachers will be excited to play with the students — and give the kids opportunities to best the teachers at different games.

- **Hours of fun:** School gaming events can provide hours of fun with an immersive gaming experience for everyone. We have surround sound and massive screens to take the gaming experience to the next level. Your students will surely be talking about any [school gaming events](/school-events/) for years to come.
