FAQs About Our Video Game Truck

Booking a video game truck is a fun and unique way to bring excitement to any gathering. Whether it's a birthday party, school event, community festival, or corporate team-building day, this mobile gaming experience provides entertainment for guests of all ages. If you're thinking about booking a video game truck for your next event, the questions below will help you get started and make the most of your rental.

  • To safely park and operate the video game truck, about 50 to 60 feet of level space is required. Suitable areas include flat driveways, parking lots, or streets that offer sufficient room and clearance for the truck, as well as easy access for guests. If you're unsure whether your space will work, it's a good idea to reach out ahead of time. Our team can review your setup and recommend the best parking option for a smooth experience.

  • Up to 23 players can comfortably enjoy the video game truck at the same time. This makes it perfect for medium to large parties or group events. For gatherings with more attendees, it's easy to create player rotations so everyone gets time to play. Our staff can help manage rotations and ensure all your guests have fun and stay engaged throughout the event.

  • A wide variety of game options are available on board, including the latest consoles, popular multiplayer titles, and even virtual reality experiences. Many companies also allow you to preview the game library in advance so you can request specific games or themes that match your event.
