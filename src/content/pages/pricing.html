Mileage Fee

Mileage fee will be included if outside of our 25-mile travel radius. If we can accommodate your same-day booking, a same-day booking fee will apply. This fee will be discussed with you prior to confirming your event.

Cancellation Policy

To secure your date and time, full payment is required along with a valid credit card. Please note, that a $99 cancellation fee will apply to any cancellations made after payment has been processed. For cancellations within 7 days of the event, no refund will be issued. However, in the case of an emergency, we will work with you to reschedule your event.
