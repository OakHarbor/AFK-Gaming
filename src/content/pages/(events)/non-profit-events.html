Educational Programs

Introducing the Power Up Program

Our Power Up Program is a 12-week interactive and educational program for elementary-aged children. Each week blends gaming, STEM education, teamwork, and creativity into engaging hands-on sessions that kids actually look forward to.

  • Teach foundational STEM concepts through interactive gaming
  • Foster teamwork, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills
  • Encourage creativity through video game design and storytelling
  • Promote healthy gaming habits and digital literacy

Our curriculum is aligned with NCSCOS standards and introduces students to real career pathways in the video game industry.
